About

The ICT Directorate office establish in the year 2009. There is recruitment of some staff on the basis of contract and assignment of some academic staff to work under the directorate office. The University is networking all of its existing campuses, interconnecting the campuses and introduction of broadband network for use both by staff and students starting to engage in applications development.

The ICT Directorate office was established to meet the following objectives:
 To plan, develop and manage University-wide ICT infrastructure
 To develop/acquire relevant application
 To manage operations and maintenance of ICT resource
 To train end users on computer literacy and offer end- user computing support
 To promote staff development program to ensure the ICT staff continue to acquire the necessary skills to provide high-quality service and support.
 To advice and consult the University management on the acquisition of ICT resource and related issues.
 Carry out researches in ICT.
